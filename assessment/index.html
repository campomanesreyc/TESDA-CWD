01. About me

Hello, I'm Rey Campomanes, an Information Technology student passionate about Web Development and UX Design. I am studying at the University of Cabuyao (Pamantasan ng Cabuyao). I am enthusiastic about exploring the different frameworks and technologies in building a web app and also the practices in UX Design.

When I'm not immersed in my studies, you might catch me exploring the captivating worlds of KDramas and Anime or delving into the fascinating stories of manhwa.

My journey in Information Technology has been a fascinating exploration of projects involving front-end development, UX design, and optimizing websites for a seamless user experience. I enjoy tackling challenges and constantly seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills.

I believe that a well-rounded individual is better equipped to tackle the complexities of the professional world. My goal is to become a developer that delivers effective IT solutions.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to learn more about my academic journey and the projects I've undertaken. If you have any questions or want to connect, please reach out here!
